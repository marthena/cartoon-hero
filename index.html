Kim Possible

About Kim

Kimberly Ann "Kim" Possible is a smart, popular and athletic high school student who moonlights as a crime-fighter, dividing much of her time between saving the world and attending cheerleading practice.

Kim's Characteristics

  1. She is super cool
  2. She is a straight A student
  3. She is strong willed and humble
  4. Protecting the worls comes naturally to her
  5. Struggles with growing up as an average teenager
